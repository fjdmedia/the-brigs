What is Brigadeiro?

Brazil's most beloved dessert.

Brigadeiro is a traditional Brazilian sweet made with cream, sweetened condensed milk, and chocolate — rolled into a ball and finished with toppings. Rich, smooth, and unlike anything else.

"The best dessert in the shape of a ball — you can have it."

Good to Know

Most of our brigadeiros are gluten-free and peanut-free. Made with milk ingredients (cream and sweetened condensed milk).

Let us know if you have a peanut or gluten allergy when you order — we'll make sure you're covered.
