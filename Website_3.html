What is Brigadeiro?

Brazil's most beloved sweet.

A classic Brazilian confection made with cream, sweetened condensed milk, and chocolate — rolled into a ball and finished with toppings. Rich, smooth, completely addictive.

Good to Know

GF

Mostly Gluten-Free

Most of our brigadeiros are gluten-free. Let us know if you have a gluten allergy when ordering.

PF

Mostly Peanut-Free

Most flavors are peanut-free. Have a peanut allergy? Just flag it when you order and we'll take care of you.
