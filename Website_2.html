What is Brigadeiro?

Brazil's favourite little treat.

Brigadeiro is a classic Brazilian sweet — made with cream, sweetened condensed milk, and chocolate, then rolled into a ball and coated in toppings. Rich, smooth, and completely addictive.

20+ Rotating Flavors

Good to Know

Most of our brigadeiros are gluten-free and peanut-free. Made with milk ingredients (cream and sweetened condensed milk).

Have a peanut or gluten allergy? Just let us know when you order.
